Thank You For Being With Me

Letter to Ananya

Dear Ananya,

Zindagi mein kuch log aise mil jaate hain jo bas milne ke liye nahi, kisi maqsad se milte hain. Tum bhi mere liye waise hi ho. Tum meri zindagi ka ek hissa nahi, meri zindagi ki wajah ban gayi ho. Shayad main lafzon mein sab kuch nahi keh sakta, lekin jo kuch mehsoos hota hai na, wo dil ke andar kahin gehra chhup gaya hai. Tumhare saath jo waqt bitaya, wo waqt nahi tha — wo meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat hissa tha. Jab tumne mujhe sirf ek boyfriend nahi, ek partner ki tarah samjha… tab maine samjha ki asli pyaar kya hota hai. Jab tumhari aankhon mein apna future dekha, tab laga ki shayad sab kuch possible hai. Par ab jab tum ja rahi ho… main tut nahi raha, bas thoda khud mein simat gaya hoon. Tumhare bina rehna mushkil hoga, par tumhare liye khush rehna meri aadat ban gayi hai. Tumse pyaar sirf ek baar nahi, har din, har pal hota hai. Chahe tum door jaa rahi ho, par tum mere dil ke sabse kareeb raho gi — hamesha. Agar kabhi kisi pal mein, kisi yaad mein, ya kisi song mein tumhe meri yaad aaye… toh sirf itna yaad rakhna — "Ek ladka tha, jo tumse har haal mein, har roop mein, bina kisi shart ke mohabbat karta tha… aur karta rahega." Tu thodi der aur thehar jaati, toh shayad waqt ruk jaata...

Hamesha tumhara,
Devansh
